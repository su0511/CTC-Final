风沙作纸，记忆为墨，献给远方的三毛
Sand for paper, memory for ink — for Sanmao.

Each time I miss you, a grain of sand falls from the sky, and thus the Sahara is formed.
每想你一次，天上飘落一粒沙，从此形成了撒哈拉。

每想你一次，
天上飘落一粒沙，
从此形成了撒哈拉。

每想你一次，
天上就掉下一滴水，
于是形成了太平洋。



Each time I miss you,
a grain of sand falls from the sky,
and thus the Sahara is formed.

Each time I miss you,
a drop of water falls from the sky,
and thus the Pacific Ocean is formed.

For in this world, I have never felt like I was one of the common masses.
因为我在这个世界上，向来不觉得是芸芸众生里的一份子。

“异乡人，走吧！”
荷西在多年前就叫我这个名字，那不是因为当时加缪的小说正在流行，那是因为“异乡人”对我来说，是一个很确切的称呼。 因为我在这个世界上，向来不觉得是芸芸众生里的一份子，我常常要跑出一般人生活着的轨道，做出解释不出原因的事情来。



“Stranger, let’s go!”
José had been calling me by this name for years. It wasn’t because Camus’s novel was popular at the time, but because *“stranger”* was truly an accurate description of me. For in this world I have never felt like I was one of the common masses; I often have to run outside the tracks of ordinary people’s lives, doing things for which I cannot explain the reason.

A life of unfettered freedom, in my view,
is the truest form of spiritual civilization.
自由自在的生活，在我的解释里，
就是精神的文明。

生命，在这样荒僻落后而贫苦的地方，一样欣欣向荣地滋长着……
我看着那些上升的炊烟，觉得他们安详得近乎优雅起来。
自由自在的生活，在我的解释里，就是精神的文明。


Life, even in such a desolate, backward and poor place, grows and flourishes with undiminished vigor…
Watching the cooking smoke rising from their homes, I felt that these people lived with a tranquility that verged on grace.
A life of unfettered freedom, in my view, is the truest form of spiritual civilization.

I cry when I want to cry,
and laugh when I want to laugh,
as long as it all springs naturally.
我爱哭的时候便哭，
想笑的时候便笑，
只要这一切出于自然。

我真心地去爱别人，因为比较不会泛滥。
我爱哭的时候便哭，想笑的时候便笑，只要这一切出于自然。

我不求深刻，只求简单。


I sincerely go about loving others, because then it’s less likely to run rampant.
I cry when I want to cry, and laugh when I want to laugh, as long as it all springs naturally.

I don’t seek to be profound; I only seek to be simple.

The journey of life—be it filled with high elegance or plain simplicity. I must taste all the flavors it offers, so that I wouldn’t have traveled this way in vain!
生命的过程，无论是阳春白雪，青菜豆腐，我都得尝尝是什么滋味，才不枉来走这么一遭。

我看着看着一张张过去的照片，丢下大叠照片，废然倒在地上，那时的心情，好似一个死去的肉体，灵魂被领到望乡台上去看他的亲人一样怅然无奈。
生命的过程，无论是阳春白雪，青菜豆腐，我都得尝尝是什么滋味，才不枉来走这么一遭啊！


As I flipped through the stack of old photographs, I let them drop and sank weakly to the floor. In that moment my mood was as forlorn and helpless as a dead soul being led to a lookout to observe his loved ones left behind. The journey of life—be it filled with high elegance or plain simplicity—I must taste all the flavors it offers, so that I wouldn’t have traveled this way in vain!

I’m thinking that when moths plunge into a flame, they must be extremely happy.
我在想，飞蛾扑火时，一定是极快乐幸福的。

吃过饭后我们在天台上坐着，那夜没有风。荷西叫我开灯，灯亮了，一群一群的飞虫马上扑过来，它们绕着光不停地打转，好似这个光是它们活着唯一认定的东西。我们两人看着这些小飞虫。

“你在想什么？”荷西问。
“我在想，飞蛾扑火时，一定是极快乐幸福的。”

After dinner we sat on the rooftop; that night there was no wind. José told me to turn on the light. As it lit up, swarms of flying insects immediately fluttered over, circling the glow incessantly as if that light were the only thing they lived for. The two of us watched these little bugs.

“What are you thinking about?” José asked.
“I’m thinking that when moths plunge into a flame, they must be extremely happy,” I replied.

If the heart has no place to rest, then wherever you go, you are merely wandering.
心若没有栖息的地方，到哪里都是在流浪。

一个人至少拥有一个梦想，有一个理由去坚强。
心若没有栖息的地方，到哪里都是在流浪。

A person should own at least one dream, a reason to stay strong.
If the heart has no place to rest, then wherever you go, you are merely wandering.
